Open Budget: Oakland visualizes the city's current and past budget data into accessible charts for the public. Data is published by the city. See Oakland's previous budgets, as well as the city's five-year forecast.

Mayor's 2019-21 Proposed Budget

The mayor's office released a proposed policy budget for fiscal years 2019 - 2020 and 2020 - 2021 in May 2019. The City Council approved a final (adopted) budget at the June 24th City Council meeting.
